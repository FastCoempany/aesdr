Section 01 — The Environment
Why Your Home Office Setup Matters

Your home office isn't just a workspace—it's the foundation of your performance. The wrong setup leads to distractions, fatigue, and burnout. A smart, intentional setup will keep you focused, healthy, and crushing your AE or SDR goals.

  • 01Focus: A chaotic space leads to distracted work and low performance.
  • 02Physical Health: Poor posture and long hours destroy your body.
  • 03Mental Clarity: A cluttered setup equals a cluttered mind.
The Reality Check
"Remote work comes with unique challenges: distractions at home, physical fatigue, and the pressure to always be 'on.'"
To succeed long-term, you need to fix these problems with a rock-solid home office setup. You cannot outwork a bad environment.

Section 02 — Setup & Blueprint
The Ultimate AE/SDR Blueprint

Building your ideal workspace requires intentionally selecting core furniture, dialing in your tech, and prioritizing your physical health.

  • 01Core Furniture: Standing desk (alternate standing and sitting), ergonomic chair with lumbar support, and 2-4 monitors raised on a desk hutch for eye-level alignment.
  • 02Technology Setup: Stockpile extra cables (HDMI, USB-C, Ethernet), add a multi-plug hub, and use a physical clock for time awareness without digital distractions.
  • 03Health and Mindset: Keep protein-packed snacks like mixed nuts, improve lighting with monitor lights or natural light, and take a short walk every 90 minutes.
The Goal
"Snacking smart, moving regularly, and having the right lighting keeps your energy high and your mind sharp."
Don't let physical fatigue kill your performance. Your environment is your competitive edge. Build a workspace that supports you, not one that distracts you.
