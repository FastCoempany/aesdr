# content/aesdr-internal/

**AESDR-internal only.** These files are NOT rendered as pages and NOT
shipped to anyone outside AESDR. They are the canonical source-of-truth
for AESDR's brand operating doctrine — voice system, full canon excerpts,
internal claims grids, full pricing math, founder-notes-laden promo
templates, day-by-day operating cadence with internal handoffs.

The principle, ratified 2026-05-09: anything we hand a partner can
plausibly leak (forwarding, screenshots, post-pilot retention). So the
threshold for what enters the gated partner kit is "does the partner
operationally need this to run a pilot?" — not "would they find this
interesting?" Brand doctrine, voice ratios, banned-vocab rationale,
operating-cadence internal handoffs — none of that helps a partner
operate. It stays here.

## Where the partner-facing content lives

- `content/partner-kit/` — public, rendered at `/partners/kit/[slug]`
- `content/partner-kit-private/` — gated, rendered at
  `/partners/kit-private/[slug]` after partner signs and is issued a
  per-partner token

This folder (`content/aesdr-internal/`) is neither — it's reference
material for AESDR's own use during copy reviews, audits, and brand
maintenance.

## Do not move files from this folder into either partner folder

Without an explicit founder review of public/gate boundaries (per the
2026-05-09 partner-kit-rebuild decision). The files in here contain
voice-system content (Rowan/Michael ratios, banned-vocab rationale, full
canon excerpts) that AESDR has decided to keep entirely internal.
