# content/partner-kit-private/

**Internal-only.** These files are NOT auto-rendered as public pages. They
contain the full partner kit (canon excerpt, claims sheet, pricing sheet,
promo templates, day-by-day cadence) and ship to partners post-signing.

The public partner kit lives in `content/partner-kit/` and is rendered at
`/partners/kit/[slug]`. The two are intentionally different artifact types:
- **Public:** partner-prospect register; positioning, lockup rules, FTC
  disclosure, curriculum overview, week-level pilot rhythm.
- **Private:** the operating manual; voice system, banned vocab, full claims
  grid, full pricing math, every promo template, day-by-day cadence.

Delivery mechanism for private docs (gated route vs. post-signing email vs.
shared drive) is TBD. Until that's decided, these files exist as the canonical
source of truth for the post-signing kit.

Do not move files from this directory into `content/partner-kit/` without an
explicit founder review of public/gate boundaries per the
2026-05-09 partner-kit-rebuild decision.
