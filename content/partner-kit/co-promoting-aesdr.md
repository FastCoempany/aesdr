# Co-Promoting AESDR

What you can say. What you can't. What we ask before you publish.

## Three bright-line rules

**1. Disclose every time.** Material connection (commission, comp, free
access) gets disclosed near the recommendation, in plain language. Full
templates by surface live in the [Disclosure Language Pack](/partners/kit/disclosure-language-pack).

**2. Don't promise outcomes we don't deliver.** AESDR is a curriculum, not
a placement service. We don't guarantee promotions, hiring, certification, or
quota attainment. If you're not sure whether a phrase crosses the line, ask
before publishing — that's faster than asking us to retroactively walk it back.

**3. Don't offer pricing your audience can't actually get.** The price is the
price. $249 SDR, $299 AE, $1,499 Team. There are no codes, no partner-stack
discounts, no flash promotions. Don't write copy that implies otherwise.

## The lockup

Co-branded surfaces (registration page, deck, partner-specific email) carry
the **AESDR × Partner** lockup. Placement, color, and clearspace rules live
in the [Lockup Usage Rules](/partners/kit/lockup-usage). The actual lockup
files arrive after the partnership agreement is signed — they're built to
your mark, so they're partner-specific.

## What's pre-cleared, what needs review

- **Pre-cleared:** language directly lifted from the kit pages and your
  promotional copy templates (sent to you after signing).
- **Needs review (48-hour round):** anything you've reworded, anything
  ad-hoc paid placement, anything ephemeral (stories, lives, reels not in
  the agreed plan).

When in doubt, send a draft. We'd rather review one extra piece than ask you
to delete a published one.

## What we won't ask of you

We won't ask you to drop your voice. The reason your audience trusts you is
that you sound like yourself. The kit gives you the language to lift, the
rules to clear, and the bright lines not to cross — everything in between is
yours.
