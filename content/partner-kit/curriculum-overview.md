# Curriculum Overview

What partners need to know about what AESDR teaches — without enrolling.

## The shape

12 courses. Sequenced from foundational to advanced. Each course pairs
operating frameworks with one or more interactive worksheets the rep keeps.
Five takeaway tools unlock across the program — practical artifacts (alignment
contract, strike plan, ROI tracker, and two more) reps use after the course
ends.

The format is interactive HTML, not video lectures. A lesson takes 15–25
minutes. You can do one on a commute, two on a slow Friday, the rest the
weekend before quarter end.

## The four arcs

The 12 courses cluster into four operating concerns reps face in their first
two years:

- **Survive ramp.** First 90 days, manager dynamics, expectation calibration.
- **Read your pipeline.** Distinguishing pipeline that closes from pipeline
  that's hope. Strike plans, deal hygiene, forecast honesty.
- **Manage the system.** Working a CRM that wasn't built for you, fighting
  for clean data, the math of your commission plan.
- **Build the next role.** Career capital, network without grovel, the
  judgment moves that compound.

## What's not in it

- Mindset work. We don't do mindset.
- Cold-call tricks. We do disqualification, not dial volume.
- Generic scripts. The lessons teach the underlying judgment; scripts you
  copy off LinkedIn rot in a quarter.
- Certificates / badges. AESDR doesn't issue one. Hiring managers want
  evidence of operating judgment, not paper.
