# Lockup Usage Rules

The **AESDR × Partner** lockup is the co-brand mark used on partner-specific
registration pages, decks, and emails. The lockup files themselves are built
to your partner mark and arrive after the partnership agreement is signed —
this page is the rules they ship with.

## Where it goes

- **Registration page:** top-center, above the fold.
- **Workshop deck:** top-left of every slide template.
- **Partner-specific email:** footer only — never the header.

The lockup signals shared authorship of the workshop. It is not a logo
placement; it's an operator credit.

## Color

- AESDR mark in `--ink` (`#1A1A1A`).
- Partner mark in your canonical color. AESDR has no opinion on yours.
- The `×` separator in `--ink` at matched x-height.

## Spacing

Minimum 24px clearspace around the lockup on all sides. Never inside a
colored panel that fights either brand. If the surface forces a tight crop,
shrink the lockup before you shrink the clearspace.

## Three things never to do

1. **No "presented by" / "in association with" / "powered by."** The lockup
   is the credit. Adding language defeats it.
2. **No third logo.** AESDR × Partner is two marks, not three. If a
   sponsor or platform mark is required by your channel, place it in a
   separate band, not inside the lockup.
3. **No proportion edits.** Don't squish, stretch, or recompose the supplied
   files. If a placement needs an aspect ratio we didn't ship, ask — we'll
   build it.

## What you'll receive after signing

Three lockup files: horizontal, stacked, and an on-cream version sized for
PDF / print use. Plus a one-page partner-specific composition guide so the
mark sits right on your registration page out of the gate.
