04 · IRIS
RESERVED · NEVER DECORATIVE

The iris gradient is the brand's heartbeat. It is rare on purpose.

linear-gradient(90deg, #FF006E 0%, #FF6B00 17%, #F59E0B 34%, #10B981 51%, #38BDF8 68%, #8B5CF6 85%, #FF006E 100%)
PERMITTED

— The brand wordmark AESDR

— The single primary CTA per surface

— Role tokens that distinguish AE / SDR

— Thin animated lines as ambient signals (≤2px)

— One iris-text payoff line per surface

— The deck numeral (only place a numeral takes iris fill)

— One accent word inside a headline (max one)

FORBIDDEN

✗ Iris-filled headlines (the whole headline)

✗ Iris backgrounds (panels, cards, surfaces)

✗ Multiple iris CTAs on the same surface

✗ Decorative flourishes (swirls, blobs, halos)

✗ Iris-filled icons (with one canonical exception: the deck numeral)

✗ Iris on body copy

✗ Iris in any retired-palette context

05 · THE TWO VOICES
CHARACTER-LED · NEVER FOUNDER-LED

Two voices, always present, never blended into mush.

ROWAN · THE VOICE THAT CLOSES
Every month, they reset your number to zero.
Your onboarding was a crime scene.
You are not building a career. You are surviving one.
Type: --cond / --display italic 900
Color: --ink on --cream, or white on --crimson
Spirit: Eli Pope. Surgical, declarative, high-status.
Move: "X is not Y. It is Z." Verdict, not argument.
MICHAEL · THE VOICE AT 2AM
My manager asked for a pipeline update. I sent a screenshot of an empty spreadsheet and wrote "minimalist aesthetic." HR laughed. But like, in a concerned way.
LinkedIn told me to "lead with value on every call." So I told a prospect about a really good taco place. Salsa rankings. They did not buy.
Type: --hand (Caveat) — and only Caveat
Color: --crimson on --cream
Spirit: Michael Scott. Confessional, deadpan, named props.
Move: Specific number → context → one-word verdict.

06 · VOICE RULES
ZERO-TOLERANCE LIST

If a piece of copy could be lifted onto a LinkedIn carousel without anyone noticing, it is wrong.

BANNED VOCABULARY

✗ "Crush it" / "crushing it" / "crush your quota"

✗ "Game-changer" / "game-changing"

✗ "Unlock your potential"

✗ "Mindset"

✗ "Rise and grind" / "grindset" / "hustle culture"

✗ "Thought leader" / "thought leadership"

✗ "Lead with value" / "trusted advisor"

✗ "Synergy" / "leverage" (as a verb)

✗ "Amazing" / "incredible" / unearned superlatives

✗ "Empower" / "empowerment"

✗ "Sales superstar" / "rockstar" / "ninja"

✗ Hype emoji (🚀💪🔥) anywhere AESDR appears

REQUIRED SIGNATURE MOVES

The verdict construction. "X is not [thing it pretends to be]. It is [what it actually is]."

The named number. Specific dollars, calls, timelines. Never "many," never "a lot."

The honest disqualification. Every offer-bearing surface names who should not buy.

Identity over technique. "We do not teach you to sell. We teach you to be the person who sells."

Cream-and-crimson visual fingerprint. Identifiable at thumbnail size.

CANONICAL TAGLINES

"The operating manual, not the motivation engine."

"Less affiliate empire. More founding vineyard."

"If you want generic sales hype, the internet has a surplus."

"We do not teach you to sell. We teach you to be the person who sells."

"Borrowed trust is a merciless mirror."

"No motivational BS. No 'crush your quota' energy."

"This isn't corporate-y but it will advance your career."
