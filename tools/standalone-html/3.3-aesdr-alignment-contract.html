Account Executive & Sales Development Alignment

This agreement outlines the operational standards, mutual expectations, and rules of engagement between the Account Executive, Jane Doe, and the Sales Development Representative, John Smith. The purpose of this document is to eliminate friction, establish clear boundaries, and maximize revenue generation.

§ I. Communication & Cadence

  • Alignment Meetings: We agree to a Weekly (30 Minutes) sync. This time is protected and will not be canceled unless mutually agreed upon.
  • Feedback Loop: Feedback on calls and meetings will be delivered directly, constructively, and within 48 hours of the event.

§ II. SDR Commitments

  • Pipeline Generation: The SDR commits to sourcing and handing over 12 highly qualified meetings per month.
  • Qualification Standard: All handed-over meetings must meet the minimum criteria defined by the BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) framework.
  • Pre-Meeting Briefs: The SDR will provide CRM notes outlining the prospect's pain points and goals prior to the AE taking the discovery call.

§ III. Account Executive Commitments

  • CRM Accountability: The AE commits to updating the CRM status of an SDR-sourced meeting within 24 Hours of the call concluding, ensuring the SDR receives accurate commission credit.
  • Mentorship: The AE agrees to spend time reviewing cold calls, offering strategic account insights, and actively aiding in the SDR's professional development.
  • No "Ghosting": The AE will not no-show an SDR-sourced meeting without securing a qualified backup to run the call.

§ IV. Rules of Engagement

Accounts over $50k ARR require AE approval before booking. SDR has autonomy on accounts under $50k.
