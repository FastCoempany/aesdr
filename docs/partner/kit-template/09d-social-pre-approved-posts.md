## What you may NOT do

- **Modify the body of any post.** The bracketed placeholders are your only edit window. Tone-shifts ("but I'd add 🔥 to the framework line") are forbidden.
- **Add a CTA / pitch** to any post that doesn't already carry one. Posts 1–3 are awareness; only post 5 and 6 carry the deadline framing.
- **Use banned vocab from §4.1** in caption text, image overlays, or alt text.
- **Repost the same post multiple times** to game the algorithm. AESDR's brand register is anti-engagement-farming per canon §1.5.
- **Boost any of these posts** without the approval round. Paid placement is canon §16 approval gate.
- **Tag AESDR's social accounts** in a way that requests reposts ("@AESDR plz RT 🙏"). Forbidden per canon §13 (no asking for borrowed amplification we haven't earned).

## What you MAY do

- **Post on a delay** so platform feeds get content evenly — schedule posts 1, 2, 3 across the promotion window rather than dumping them.
- **A/B-style** between posts 1 and 2 if your platform analytics support it. Lift either; results are inputs to D33 postmortem.
- **Translate platform-naturally** — Twitter/X variants may need to be split across thread; LinkedIn may carry the full text. The body stays the same; the rendering is yours.
- **Reply substantively** to comments under your post, in your own voice, within canon §4.1 vocab limits. AESDR welcomes the operator-to-operator engagement.
