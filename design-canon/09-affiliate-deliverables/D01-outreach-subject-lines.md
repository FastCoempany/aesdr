# D01 — Outreach Subject Lines

**Deliverable:** 20 subject lines for partner outreach (cold + bumps).
**Audience:** Community operators, bootcamp coaches, micro-creators, alumni ambassadors.
**Voice ratio:** 80 Rowan / 20 Michael. Operator-to-operator, not LinkedIn-formal.
**Canon refs:** `AFFILIATE_BRAND_CANON.md` §3.3 (voice ratios), §4.4 (subject-line discipline), §5 (anti-LinkedIn).

## Discipline

- 4–8 words.
- No emoji. No `Re:` tricks (unless it actually is a reply). No ALL CAPS.
- No curiosity gaps ("you won't believe…").
- Lowercase or sentence-case only.
- Each subject is a contract — the body must deliver in sentence one.

## A. Cold to community operators (5)

1. Workshop pilot for early-career reps in your community
2. One live workshop, your audience, honest numbers
3. Founding pilot — non-exclusive, time-boxed
4. Built for the reps you already host
5. Tight fit — pilot for [community] members

## B. Cold to bootcamp coaches (5)

6. After placement, before confident ramp
7. Co-host a workshop for your alumni
8. Pilot for first-two-year reps in your network
9. Workshop-first, no exclusivity, your alumni
10. Short, tracked, honest — alumni pilot

## C. Cold to micro-creators / alumni ambassadors (5)

11. Your audience looks unusually relevant
12. Founding pilot — one workshop, one offer
13. Pilot fit — early-career reps in your circle
14. Small pilot, real tracking, your room
15. One workshop, your community, no carousel

## D. Bumps / replies to silence (5)

16. Bumping once, then I'll go quiet
17. Re: workshop pilot — still open
18. Quick note on the pilot
19. One more nudge, then quiet
20. Last ping on the pilot

## Notes

- `[community]`, `[partner]`, etc. are placeholders — fill before send. Never send a templated bracket.
- "No carousel" (#15) is the one Michael-flavored line in the set; it carries the anti-LinkedIn signal without being mean about it.
- D-bucket (bumps) is meant to be deployed *once*. If a partner doesn't reply to the bump, we move on — `we part as adults`.
- Test set: send 3 from each bucket A–C in the first wave, measure reply rate by archetype, retire dead lines, replace with new ones in v2.

## Open

- Subject-line A/B variants for the same partner (do we test, or send the best line?). Default: send the best line; we don't have the volume to A/B yet.
- Partner-name personalization in subject (e.g., "[community] members" vs generic): default is partner-name in body, generic in subject — saves us when the subject auto-truncates.
