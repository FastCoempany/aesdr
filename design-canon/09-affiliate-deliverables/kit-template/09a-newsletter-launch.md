## What lands well in pilots (founder notes)

- **Personal opener that names a specific reason.** "I came across AESDR because [specific moment with your audience]" beats "I'm excited to announce" by every metric.
- **Forwarding it to one person you have in mind.** A 100-recipient send + one 1:1 forward to a member who's been asking exactly this question outperforms a 100-recipient send alone.
- **Plain text over heavy design.** AESDR's brand is type and operating-doctrine register; a richly-designed launch email reads as off-brand even when the content is right.
- **Resending to non-openers** *only* with a clean subject swap and the same body. Not in this template — the reminder send is `09b`.
