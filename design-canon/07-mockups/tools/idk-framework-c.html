The IDK Framework

When you don't know the answer, own it like a professional.

The Golden Rule
"I don't know — but I will find out and get back to you by [specific time]."
1

Identify the Gap

Acknowledge what you don't know without apologizing excessively. Confidence comes from honesty, not omniscience.

"That's a great question. I want to make sure I give you the most accurate answer rather than guess."
2

Deploy the Bridge

Redirect with value. Share what you DO know, then commit to a specific follow-up timeline.

"What I can tell you is [related value point]. For the specifics on [their question], I'll loop in our solutions engineer and have a detailed answer by end of day tomorrow."
3

Keep the Promise

Follow through BEFORE your stated deadline. This builds more trust than having the answer in the first place.

"Hi [Name], following up as promised. I spoke with our team and here's the detailed answer to your question about [topic]..."
