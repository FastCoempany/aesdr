AE–SDR Alignment Contract

A Working Agreement Between Partners in Revenue

Effective: April 21, 2026  |  Review Cycle: Bi-weekly

§ I. Parties & Purpose

1.1 This agreement is entered between Jordan Kim (Account Executive) and Alex Chen (Sales Development Representative) for the purpose of establishing clear expectations, communication rhythms, and mutual accountability.

1.2 Both parties acknowledge that pipeline is a shared responsibility, and that the AE-SDR relationship is the atomic unit of revenue generation.

§ II. Meeting Cadence & Communication

2.1 Sync meetings shall occur bi-weekly on Tuesdays at 9:00 AM.

2.2 Response time SLA: Slack messages answered within 2 hours during business hours. 4 hours

Changed from 4h → 2h per Q2 retro feedback

2.3 Meeting prep: Both parties will review shared pipeline doc before each sync. No cold meetings.

§ III. Lead Qualification Criteria

3.1 A meeting is considered "qualified" when it meets the following minimum criteria:

a) Decision-maker or strong influencer confirmed
b) Budget range identified: $50K+ ACV
c) Timeline: active evaluation within 90 days
d) Pain point articulated (not just "interested in learning more")

This is the bar. Below this = nurture, not a meeting.

3.2 Meetings that do not meet criteria in 3.1 are logged as "discovery calls" and do not count toward SDR quota.

§ IV. Feedback & Dispute Resolution

4.1 AE shall provide written feedback on every meeting within 24 hours of completion: Qualified / Not Qualified / Needs Follow-up.

4.2 Disputes on qualification shall be resolved in the next sync. If unresolved, escalate to Sales Manager.

4.3 Neither party shall "go around" the other to management without first attempting direct resolution. This is a partnership, not a hierarchy.

§ V. Signatures & Commitment

By signing below, both parties commit to honoring this agreement and revisiting it at each bi-weekly sync for amendments.
