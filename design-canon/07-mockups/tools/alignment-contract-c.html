AE-SDR Alignment Contract

A mutual commitment to pipeline excellence

Parties

This contract is entered between Jordan Mitchell (Account Executive) and Alex Chen (Sales Development Representative), operating within the Mid-Market West segment.

Pipeline Commitments

The SDR commits to delivering 12 qualified meetings per month that meet the agreed-upon ICP criteria. The AE commits to attending all scheduled meetings on time and providing actionable feedback within 24 hours.

Ideal Customer Profile

  • Title: VP Sales or above
  • Company size: 200-2000 employees
  • Industry: SaaS / Technology
  • Pain signals: Scaling outbound, hiring SDRs, quota attainment below 70%

Communication & Feedback

Both parties agree to a Weekly 1:1 (15 min) cadence. Feedback must be specific, constructive, and documented. No ghosting on Slack threads related to active prospects.

Qualification Standards

  • Budget: Confirmed or inferable from company stage
  • Authority: Contact is decision-maker or confirmed champion
  • Need: Explicit pain stated in discovery or trigger event documented
  • Timeline: Active evaluation within 90 days

Special Agreements

AE commits to same-day feedback on all meetings booked. SDR gets first right on any expansion opps from sourced accounts.

Accountability

If either party fails to meet their commitments for two consecutive weeks, a reset conversation is triggered with management present. This contract will be reviewed and renewed monthly.
