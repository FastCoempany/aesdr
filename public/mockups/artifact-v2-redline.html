The Accepted Manuscript

Accepted Manuscript
The version that survives the edit.
Pipeline
Before passing any lead, confirm all four BANT criteria. If I can't answer one, ask the question instead of hoping the AE will figure it out.
Discipline
8:00–9:30 is prospecting. No Slack, no email, no CRM. Calendar blocked, phone on DND. Every day, no exceptions.
Coaching
Every Friday, ask my AE for one specific piece of feedback. Cite the exact call or email. Stop waiting to be told.
Networking
One meaningful LinkedIn conversation per week. Not a connection request — a real conversation. Build the network before I need it.
The first draft was who you think you are. The redline is who you actually are. The accepted manuscript is who you're choosing to become.
