AESDR Lesson 12 Takeaway
Lesson 12.3 — Action Plan
72-Hour Strike Plan
Three days. No mercy. Pick your battles and execute — or nothing from this course mattered.
1
No more than 3 actions per day. Focus beats volume.
2
Every action must have a deliverable — not "try harder."
3
Debrief each night. Adjust. Then attack again.
