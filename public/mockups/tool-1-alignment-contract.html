I. Pipeline Commitments
  • SDR will deliver a minimum of 5 qualified meetings per week to AE.
  • All leads must meet BANT qualification standard (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) before handoff.
  • SDR will include a 2-sentence qualification summary with every meeting invite.
II. Feedback & Coaching
  • AE will provide structured feedback via weekly 1:1 (30 min).
  • Feedback will reference specific calls, emails, or leads — not generalities.
  • SDR may request ad-hoc feedback on any lead within 24 hours of handoff.
III. Mutual Accountability
  • AE will shadow 2 SDR calls per month.
  • SDR will attend 1 AE demo per week for learning.
  • Both parties review pipeline together every Friday at 3pm.
IV. Escalation Protocol
If either party fails to meet commitments for two consecutive weeks, the other may request a facilitated conversation with their shared manager. The goal is resolution, not punishment.
